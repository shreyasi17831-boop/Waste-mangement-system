Waste Mangement System

Developed a campus-based waste management system to monitor and manage the cleanliness of waste bins efficiently.
Users can capture live photos of bins, and the system categorizes their fill level using color indicators (green, yellow, red).
Based on the bin status, cleaning tasks are automatically assigned to maintenance staff.
Workers upload completion photos after cleaning, ensuring transparency and real-time updates for users.
The system also integrates GPS and Google Maps to verify on-campus locations and display bin positions for better monitoring.
